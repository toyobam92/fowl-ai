Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Effective date: May 2026

Who we are

FOWL AI ("we", "us", "our") publishes a free weekly newsletter and digital guides about AI training platforms, AI jobs, and career strategy for knowledge workers.

What data we collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to the FOWL AI newsletter via our subscribe form. This is processed by Beehiiv, our newsletter platform.
  • Purchase data — when you purchase a digital product, payment and delivery is handled entirely by Gumroad. We receive your email address from Gumroad for the purpose of delivering your purchase and sending product updates.
  • Website analytics — we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site. No personal data is stored by us directly.

How we use your data

  • To send you the FOWL AI newsletter (if you subscribed)
  • To deliver digital products you have purchased
  • To send product updates or corrections when relevant
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Referral and affiliate links

Some links on this site, particularly on the Platforms page, are referral links. FOWL AI may receive a small commission if you sign up for a platform through these links, at no additional cost to you. These relationships do not influence our editorial content or rankings.

Cookies

This site does not set cookies directly. Newsletter and payment services used may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (unsubscribing from the newsletter deletes your subscriber record)
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, use the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email or contact us via the subscribe page.

Data retention

We retain subscriber email addresses for as long as you remain subscribed to the newsletter. You may request deletion at any time by unsubscribing.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
