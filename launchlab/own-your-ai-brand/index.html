FAQ

Questions answered.

Do I need to know how to code? +
No. This kit is designed to be beginner friendly. Every workflow is explained step by step, and the Builder Pack includes templates and prompts that work without any technical background. If you can use a word processor and copy-paste a prompt, you can follow this kit.
Can I use ChatGPT instead of Claude? +
Yes. The kit covers both Claude and ChatGPT in the tool comparison chapter, and the prompt library includes versions for both. The underlying principles work regardless of which AI you prefer — we teach the system, not a specific platform.
Can I use different AI tools? +
Absolutely. The kit presents multiple implementation options for every workflow — from free beginner-friendly tools to more advanced alternatives. The resource library includes a full comparison so you can make an informed decision based on your budget and goals.
Can I use a different avatar platform? +
Yes. The kit covers the most widely used AI avatar and video platforms with honest comparisons of their costs, quality, and limitations. You'll have enough information to choose the platform that fits your workflow — or to switch later as new tools emerge.
How long does this take? +
Most people using the Quick Start workflow publish their first AI video within a weekend. Reading the full Playbook takes 3–4 hours. Working through the full Builder Pack end to end typically takes 1–2 weeks depending on how much time you dedicate — but you can publish before you finish.
Will this continue to work as AI evolves? +
Yes — because the kit teaches principles, not just platform steps. The specific tools will change. The underlying logic of building a brand identity, defining a voice, and creating a repeatable content workflow will not. Builder Pack purchasers also receive all future updates as the kit evolves with new tools and workflows.
